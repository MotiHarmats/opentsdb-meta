Module fasthash::t1ha::t1ha2[−][src]

t1ha2 = 64 and 128-bit, SLIGHTLY MORE ATTENTION FOR QUALITY AND STRENGTH.

  • The recommended version of "Fast Positive Hash" with good quality for checksum, hash tables and fingerprinting.
  • Portable and extremely efficiency on modern 64-bit CPUs. Designed for 64-bit little-endian platforms, in other cases will runs slowly.
  • Great quality of hashing and still faster than other non-t1ha hashes. Provides streaming mode and 128-bit result.
